A Botswana-wide hackathon hosted at Botho University

March 16, 8:00am to 5:00pm

March 17, 8:00am to 6:00pm

A hackathon is a 2-day creative marathon where students come together and use their skills to take on real-world technical challenges and projects. In groups of 1 to 4, students will come up with an idea, and over the course of the hackathon, work together to make it a reality. At BothoHacks, we'll provide meals, mentorships, workshops and more! We just want you to come with your computer and a good attitude.
